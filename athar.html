About Athar Award


Coinciding with the 5th anniversary of the company and Egypt CSR & Sustainable Development Forum, CSR Egypt is delighted to launch the 1st edition of “Athar Award” to celebrate and reward the best CSR and Sustainability practices and initiatives in Egypt. The awards ceremony will be held on the sideline of the “10th Egypt CSR and Sustainable Development Forum on 6 April 2020.

The award is dedicated for the private sector, NGOs, entrepreneurs, academic institutions and individuals to honor their inspiring programs, projects and initiatives that focus on the essential aspects of sustainability and CSR, and in the same time have remarkable social impact.

“Athar Award” is reflecting our company’s values and goals to raise awareness of the importance of corporates and organizations participation in achieving sustainable development and Egypt vision 2030.

The award also aims to give the deserved recognition and exposure to the key market players and the most influential entities in achieving CSR and sustainability, and highlighting their efforts in transforming the Egyptian business environment and enriching the national development strategy in the past few years.

“Athar Award” provides you with the opportunity to sparkle, promote, network and share your success story with hundreds of government representatives, business owners, NGOs, experts, entrepreneurs, startups and CSR & Sustainability stakeholders in Egypt and Arab world.
